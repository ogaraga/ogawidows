Crisis Management process.

The control or management of crisis means much more than the problem itself, hence this department. We do not wait for crisis to happen before acting. We act before it happens.

Crisis Phases

There are about 3 phases to crisis control namely: pre-crisis:The first step in resolving a crisis is preventing it from happening. This involves creating a department for this kind of situation and hiring and training volunteers/staff to develop and implement a crisis plan. That is what we do here. Also, it is essential to have a clear communication for everyone to inform them about the situation and not creating a panic situation. Pre-written crisis messages, information or jingles helps get our team prepared ahead thereby saving time resolving any crisis event when it occurrs. Crisis management & response:The second step of the process is to directly handle and manage the problem. This stage is when we implement developed plans. Crisis messages are released, and employees are in contact and always updated on the situation.Our crisis team must resolve the problem before the damage increases. Post-crisis: When we pass through the crisis successfully. It is imperative to remain in contact with your employees, clients, etc. to announce that the storm is over and that everything is normal. After the crisis has been resolved, we try to answer questions from everyone interested in our projects. Finally, we endeavour to conclude with our crisis team on the response rate and areas that need immediate modifications for better performance in subsequence happenings.

Types of crises

Financial crisis: Experiencing decline in winning overseas or local partnership/grants can affect financial box of the organization. Also, inefficient and ineffective handling/execution of projects could lead to decline in getting contracts, hence financial down-times. We strive to be professional and technical in implementing all won contracts. We have zero tolerance to fraud and stealing. All financial report must be done very accountably and professionally well.

Personnel crisis: This occurs when employees or someone associated with the organisation is involved in an action that is considered unethical, illegal, or harmful for the business.We have had this issue in the past, but a lot of changes have taken place where this department ensures tenecious accountability.

Organizational crisis: This happens when a brand is engaging wrongfully with the customers by taking actions that impact clients negatively. An example of this situation is represented by keeping away important information from clients when they deserve to know what they are investing in. We have a clean sheet here.

Technological crisis: We are just getting our website on the net. We have not had any server issues or data related compromise even though we have technicl team that sees to the security of our server and database.

Natural crisis: Our organisation is located in a good space with great natural blessing. No hurricane, tornado, vulcanic erruptions, flood, etc so far.
